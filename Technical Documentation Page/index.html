Company Description

One Stop Bike Shop

The name of the company will be "One Stop”. We came up with the bike shop business idea due to the pandemic. Since the start of the pandemic, many people started buying bikes due to the lack of public transportation. It is also one of the new trend hobbies of many people. This caused a great rise in the demand for bike parts and bike services.

The goal of our business is to be the same as its name. We aim to be a one stop shop where the bikers can get their bike parts and services regardless of their available budget. We will offer services and products to our normal bikers, everyday workers, and even professional athletes. Our business offers fully built bikes for the use of everyone, different bike equipment’ s for the safety and efficiency of our riders, and Bike parts are also available in case of upgrades or replacement. We also offer maintenance and repair services to ensure the functionality and durability of the bikes.

Market Analysis

MEMBERSHIPS

Study shows that memberships are the new loyalty in retail these days, statistically, people will tend to buy with the shops they have memberships in (i.e. Landers, S&R, etc.) 47% of the survey ants say that they would pay more for enhanced benefits rather than shopping and buying in other non-membership stores for free. Although our membership would not be mandatory in buying at our store, you would get exclusive access to promos, products, and services like the points system that we would offer.


TRADE IN SERVICE

Trade in services are being planned to be offered at our store. Since it would widen the selection to cheaper second hand products, and could also attract customers who might want to upgrade with an added discount for their leftover parts. Statistically speaking about 80% of consumers get attracted and buy discounted prices. And that is exactly what we are going to offer here. Discounts for their purchases in exchange for parts.


ONLINE PLATFORM

We also plan on establishing an online platform to sell our products, like Shopee or Lazada. About 85% of the earth' s population has been buying online since the start of the pandemic and that number grows each year. So here are some ways an online platform could be beneficial to our business,

First is we establish a new way of selling our products, it is much convenient for the customer to shop online rather than coming in the house. the second way is we expand our market. Thru an online platform, we could reach people nationwide as supposed to the people in the vicinity of our shop, therefore increasing our target demographic which is bikers. And last but not least is that in an online platform, store hours don 't matter. They can shop in the middle of the night and place their order. Then we would package and send it to them during store hours.
